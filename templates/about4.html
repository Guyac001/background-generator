03 : About me

Born in Brum', raised in Bromsgrove...

Born in Colmore, central Birmingham, just a long Goal Kick from Aston Villa's grounds, raised in the idyllic outskirts of Bromsgrove, Privately Educated until... State Schooled from '69.

Teenage Life, Sun and Fun by the Sea...

Moved to Gosport in early 1970, Leesland Junior School... then the First Year through the new Comprehensive System of 1972... from 'Fatty' to lanky and fit from Swimming & Cycling...

Mis-spent Years & the end of School

Moved to Banbury in late 1975, Stanbridge Hall, part of the Biggest Comprehensive School in the Country... 3,000 pupils; a melting-pot of influences, a confluence of kids from all over the Country. Bitten by the 'music bug', then, overwhelmed by education and family tragedy, a feeling of utter failure with the A-level results in the Summer of '79.

Re-Birth and Re-Education

Free from School and the dread of endless Schooling, worked as a Draughtsman / Buyer, later a Gravedigger, upgrading CSE Grade 1s to O-levels that would allow entry to Further Education. Studied new A-levels at North Oxfordshire Technical College. Studied for an Honours Maths Degree at the former Hatfield Polytechnic, now re-badged as The University of Hertfordshire

Sandwich Year & the Final Crust

Split the Maths Degree with a 'sandwich year' at Culham Laboratory (JET: the Joint European Torus, the UK Atomic Energy Authority's Atomic Fusion Research Centre), programming Fortran in conjunction with Graphics Libraries, cutting edge at the time, then returned for a Final Year concentrating on 'The Maths of Computing' and studying the early roots of Object Oriented Programming with the OBJ language, again, cutting edge at the time.
